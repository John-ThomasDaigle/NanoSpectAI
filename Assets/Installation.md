Step 1: Copy Whisper onto Jetson Orin Nano using this repository: https://github.com/ZGMFX20AR/audio-transcription-using-openai-whisper

Step 2: Download NanoSpectAITkinter.py + NanoSpectAICamera.py + NanoSpectAIMobile.py onto the Jetson Orin Nano

Step 3: Download mobile app (NanoSpectAIMobile) on your Android device and connect the device to the Jetson via Bluetooth

Step 4: Connect your USB microphone to the Jetson

Step 5: Boot the machine, or run either script after boot. Take pictures with Android mobile device and speak into the microphone. After finishing, press stop on the mobile app to terminate the script, or pres ESC on the Jetson if you are using the USB camera and allow the program to finish transcribing. 

Step 7: Install all files in NanoSpectAI-UI. Into the UI, input document from inspection, along with building type, date of inspection, address, cover photo, logo, and inspector photos. 

The program will generate a full inspection report based off of your inputted document.
